Pizza Recipe

Description:

The homemade pizza is a staple in any house chefs arsenal. While there are many ways to make a pizza, it is better to make one that utilizes ancient techniques that bring out the most flavor from the ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Bread Flour: (X grams)
  • Water: (75-80 % of X grams)
  • Salt: (2 % of X grams)
  • Active Yeast: (1 % of X grams)
  • Cheese (Mozzarela, ideally low moisture)
  • San Marzano tomatoe, crushed
  • Olive oil
  • Honey or Sugar (1.5% of X)

Steps:

  1. Create a prefermented base:
    1. In a bowl, add in all the water you will use with all the honey or sugar the recipe calls for
    2. Mix the ingredients in the bowl and let sit for 10 min for the yeast to activate
    3. Once activated, mix in EQUAL PARTS BREAD FLOUR TO WATER and let sit for 1 hour
    4. After letting the dough sit for one hour, place it in the fridge and let it sit for 24 hours

  2. Creating the Dough
    1. Mix in the remaining bread flour, it should be the remaining 15-20 % by weight that was not used
    2. Add in the salt
    3. Combine the dough together by using the slap and fold technique until the dough is smooth (5-10 minutes)
    4. Apply olive oil to a bowl, and place across the dough
    5. Transfer dough into bowl
    6. Let sit for 1-2 hours until the volume doubles
    7. Remove the dough from the bowl, and split into four Equal sized pieces
    8. Tighten the dough balls and let them sit for 30 minutes for a final proof

  3. Molding the Pizza and Baking
    1. Take one piece of the devided bread dough and flatten it out using fingertips
    2. Place crushed san marzano tomatoes and mozzarela cheese
    3. Place additional toppings, and finish off by drizzling olive oil
    4. Place into a pizza stone, or pizza steel that has been inside an oven that has been preheated to 500Â°F
    5. After 10 minutes remove and serve after cooling down.
